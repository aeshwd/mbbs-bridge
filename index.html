Frequently Asked Questions

1. Is this a consultancy or admission agent?

No. This platform is completely independent and student-led. We do not sell seats, push consultants, or take commissions.

2. Do I have to pay to get guidance here?

Absolutely not. All guidance, videos, and advice are 100% free. There are no hidden charges.

3. Can parents contact you for advice?

Yes. Parents are encouraged to ask questions. We provide honest guidance for both students and parents.

4. Are the YouTube videos and testimonials real?

Yes. All videos are created by MBBS students, and testimonials are shared by real students with their consent. No fake reviews or marketing content is used.

5. How can I submit my questions or doubts?

You can use the contact form at the bottom of the page. Enter your name, WhatsApp number, and question. Our student mentors will answer freely and honestly.
